Run the brand-kit-capture onboarding workflow. Captures tagline, brand text + optional separator styling, 8 brand colors (with defaults derived if incomplete), display + mono fonts (validated against Google Fonts), and optional logo / wordmark mark / headshot asset files. Persists to `~/.config/realty-stack/brand-kit.md` and `~/.config/realty-stack/brand-assets/` on approval.

Full workflow documented at `${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/skills/brand-kit-capture/SKILL.md` — follow it exactly.
